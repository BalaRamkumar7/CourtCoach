Our mission — a work in progress

Coaching shouldn't be a privilege.

CourtCoach exists to put real coaching in reach of anyone with a phone. Our give-back plan takes that one step further: helping fund basketball coaching for kids who can't access it at all — in underserved communities across the US and around the world.

Status: in planning — no commitments finalized yet
The idea

Talent is everywhere. Coaching isn't.

In a lot of neighborhoods — and a lot of countries — kids grow up playing basketball but rarely get taught how to actually get better. No coach, no feedback, no path. That gap is exactly what CourtCoach was built to close, and we don't want to stop at the players who can already reach us.

So the plan is simple: use CourtCoach to help fund the people already doing this work on the ground — organizations that put real coaches and real programs in front of kids who'd otherwise go without.

The plan

What we're working toward.

01

Direct a share of contributions

We intend to channel a meaningful, fixed share of the contributions CourtCoach receives toward youth-coaching programs — rather than keeping it all in the app.

02

Partner with a vetted nonprofit

We're identifying and vetting a nonprofit partner already delivering basketball coaching to underserved kids, in the US and internationally — real programs, real reach, real transparency.

03

Say exactly what we do

Once it's set, we'll publish the specifics — who the partner is, how much goes to them, and how the money flows — and report the totals over time.

To be clear

Nothing here is final yet. We haven't committed to a percentage, announced a partner, or started moving money. This page is our intention — we'd rather share the plan honestly than make a promise we can't yet keep.

How we'll do it right

The principles guiding this.

Transparency first

When it's live, we'll show where the money goes and publish running totals. No vague "a portion of proceeds."

Real programs

Funds go to organizations delivering actual coaching and access on the ground — not overhead, not middlemen.

Honest words

We won't call something a tax-deductible donation unless it truly is, and we'll be clear about what's charitable and what keeps the app running.

Local & global

Talent doesn't care about zip codes or borders. Our goal is to reach kids both in underserved US cities and abroad.

Where we are today

Early, but serious.

This is a plan we're actively building, not a live program — we're in the partner-vetting stage right now. If you want to watch it take shape (and be there when CourtCoach launches), the best way to follow along is the waitlist.
