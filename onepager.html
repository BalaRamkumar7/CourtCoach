CourtCoach
Partnership Overview
Prepared for PeacePlayers

Great coaching, for every kid — not just the lucky ones.

CourtCoach is an AI basketball coaching app. It uses a phone's camera to read a player's form in real time and coach them like a real trainer would — the why and the fix, rep by rep. Our reason for building it is access: quality coaching shouldn't depend on where you're born or what you can afford.

How it works
01

Point the camera

Prop up a phone, pick a drill. No wearables, no coach, no setup.

02

AI reads your form

Live pose tracking measures the joint angles that make or break a shot.

03

Coaching, out loud

A spoken tip on every rep, then a full breakdown after the session.

Where we are
Web & mobile in active development AI form-tracking prototype built Metrics grounded in biomechanics research (published openly) Waitlist open Funding model: user contributions
Why we're reaching out

We want to put our mission where our mouth is. CourtCoach is proposing to direct a defined, meaningful share of the contributions we receive to PeacePlayers — and to feature your programs inside the app and on our site so supporters know exactly where their impact goes.

PeacePlayers is the natural fit: you use basketball to reach kids in underserved communities across both the US and internationally — exactly the players CourtCoach exists to serve. We're early-stage and want to set this up transparently and correctly from day one, in whatever structure works best on your end.
